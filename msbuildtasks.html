MSBuild Tasks

The MSBuild tasks of Appccelerate are little helpers to make development easier.

They are executed as part of a build. Therefore, no additional scripting is needed.

All tasks can be installed into your projects with Nuget.

Check out the MSBuild tasks (navigation on the right).
